Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Microsoft Office installation, activation, and troubleshooting.

How do I install Microsoft Office remotely?

We provide remote installation services via secure remote access. Our experts guide you through the setup, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free installation process.

What is included in your Microsoft Office activation service?

Our activation service includes verifying your Office license, activating the software with a genuine key, and resolving any activation errors you may encounter.

What should I do if Microsoft Office is not opening?

If your Office apps fail to open, try restarting your system, updating Office, or repairing the installation. If the issue persists, our troubleshooting experts can assist you remotely.

Can I transfer my Office license to another laptop?

Yes, if you have a transferable license (e.g., Microsoft 365 or a retail license), you can deactivate Office on the old device and reinstall it on the new one. We can assist with the process.

How do I fix Microsoft Office activation errors?

Common activation errors include incorrect product keys, expired subscriptions, and connectivity issues. We diagnose and resolve these problems for a seamless activation experience.

Do you provide support for Microsoft 365 subscription issues?

Yes! We help with Microsoft 365 setup, subscription renewals, account management, and troubleshooting any subscription-related issues.
